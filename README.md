# PaceWise - Long COVID Energy Management Assistant

PaceWise is a fully client-side, single-file web app that helps people living with Long COVID practice safe energy pacing. It tracks daily symptoms, activity load, and recovery patterns - then surfaces personalized insights to help users stay within their energy envelope and avoid post-exertional malaise (PEM). 

It is built as a single HTML file with no backend, no accounts, and no data leaving your device.

## Features:

Daily Check-in: Log energy, fatigue, brain fog, and pain levels (0-10) with smooth sliders. Mark days as Crash, Flare, or Good. Add free-text notes.

Energy Budget System: Set a daily energy limit and log activities with assigned cost weights (walking = 2, desk work = 5, exercise = 7, etc.). A live ring gauge shows remaining capacity and warns when you're near or over budget.

Insights & Trend Charts: Visualize energy trends, symptom severity, and daily activity load over 7, 14, or 30 days using interactive Chart.js graphs.

Pattern Detection: Automated analysis detects activity–fatigue correlations, brain fog spikes after over-budget days, energy streaks, crash frequency, and day-of-week patterns.

Timeline History: A searchable, sortable, filterable history of every logged day - with expandable cards showing full symptom metrics, activity chips, budget bars, and notes. Filter by crash days, flare days, or over-budget days.

Baseline Tracking: Computes your 30-day energy baseline and shows recent deviations - so you can tell when you're trending below your personal norm.

Recovery Tracking: Tracks how many days it takes to recover after each crash, with averages and longest recovery logged.

AI-Style Weekly Summary: Generates a natural-language report covering average energy, worst symptom, crash/flare count, over-budget days, trend direction, and a personalized pacing suggestion.

Pacing Recommendations: Context-aware tips update dynamically based on your current energy and load - from rest-day guidance to the energy envelope method.

Doctor Summary & Export: Copy a formatted plain-text report to share with your care team, or download your full data as JSON or CSV.

Dark / Light Mode: Toggle between deep aurora dark mode and a soft light mode, with preference saved across sessions.

Reminder Notifications: Optional browser notifications to prompt your daily check-in.

Getting Started: No installation needed.

## Privacy: 

All data stays in your browser. Nothing is transmitted, stored on a server, or shared with any third party. Clearing your browser data will erase your logs - use the Export feature to back up regularly.

## Disclaimer: 

PaceWise is a personal tracking tool, not a medical device. It does not provide medical advice. Always consult your healthcare team for clinical decisions about your Long COVID management.
