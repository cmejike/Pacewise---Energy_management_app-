PaceWise
Understand your energy. Protect your limits. Prevent crashes.

Long COVID pacing is the practice of staying within your energy limits to avoid post-exertional malaise (PEM) — the crash that follows overexertion. PaceWise helps you track, understand, and act on your daily energy so you can do more of what matters, safely.
